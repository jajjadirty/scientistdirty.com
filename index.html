DIGESTION

This refers to the breakdown of complex food particles into simple soluble compounds that can be absorbed and assimilated by the body.

Their are two types of digestion namely;

Mechanical digestion
Chemical digestion

Mechanical digestion.

This is the breakdown of food by the help of the teeth but not enzymes.
  • This only takes place in the mouth.

Chemical digestion.

This is the breakdown of food due to enzyme actoin.
  • This is the type of digestion which takes place in almost all parts of the system.

Note

Extracellular digestion is the digestion in man

Extracellular digestion is when digestion takes place out side the body cells.

NB

This may not necessarily be outside the body cells but it may occur inside the body but not in the cells.

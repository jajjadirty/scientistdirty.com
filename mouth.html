MOUTH

Wen food is taken in, the mouth opens the large space called buccal cavity then the teeth breaks down food particles by a process called mechanical digestion.

This creats a large surface area for enzyme action

Chemical digestion in the mouth.

The saliva contains an enzyme called salivary amylase which changes starch into maltose (ptyalin).

This enzyme works only on starch.

Examples of food that contain starch.

  • Cassava
  • Sweet potato
  • Matooke
  • Yams
